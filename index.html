About Philosophy School


Philosophy School is a learning space dedicated to exploring fundamental questions about life, existence, knowledge, and human thought.
It focuses on developing critical thinking, logical reasoning, and deeper understanding of how we interpret reality.
The purpose of this school is not to provide fixed answers, but to encourage students to question assumptions, analyze ideas,
and think independently. Through discussions on philosophy, ethics, and different schools of thought such as existentialism,
stoicism, and absurdism, students learn how to reason clearly and express ideas with confidence. Philosophy School aims to train
the mind to think beyond surface-level understanding and to engage with complex ideas in a structured way.
